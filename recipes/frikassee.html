Hühnerfrikassee

Description

This dish is a german dish. It has a slighly sour and creamy sauce containing a handful of diverse vegetables with chicken. Germans eat this dish with rice on a single plate. My friend in Aachen once told me that they always eat this dish at christmas.

Ingredients

  • carrots
  • brown mushrooms
  • cauliflower
  • asparagus
  • green peas
  • tomato
  • lemon
  • chicken meat
  • rice
  • cream
  • onion
  • flour
  • butter
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in a rice cooker
  2. Wash the vegetables and the meat
  3. Quarter tomatoes and the onion
  4. To make the broth, bring 1 liter of water to boil and add the tomatoes, onions and chicken
  5. Let it simmer for around 30 min, until the meat is done
  6. In the meantime, cut the rest of the vegetables into small pieces
  7. Strain the broth with a colander and cut or pluck the meat. You can toss the cooked tomatoes and onion
  8. Make a roux with butter and flour. Once thick, pour the broth into the roux and stir frequently to make a thick sauce
  9. Add the vegetables and the meat to the sauce and cook for about 20 min
  10. Add a little bite of cream
  11. Season with salt, pepper and lemon juice to your liking
  12. Done! Serve it with rice
