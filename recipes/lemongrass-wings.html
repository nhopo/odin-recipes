Fried Spicy Lemongrass Chicken Wings

Description

This is a homemade dish my mother created. In vietnamese cuisine, we like to cook with lemongrass. This dish is fairly simple, but tastes good either with rice or as finger food!

Ingredients

  • chicken wings
  • fresh chili
  • fresh lemongrass
  • salt and pepper
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cut the chili and lemongrass into small rings
  2. Rinse the chicken
  3. If the chicken comes in a whole piece, cut it in the middle to seperate drums from stick
  4. Put the pieces into a bowl and season with salt and pepper
  5. Deep fry the chicken wings in hot oil. If you have an air fryer you can also use this instead
  6. As soon as the chicken is done and has a golden color, toss them in a pan together with lemongrass and chili
