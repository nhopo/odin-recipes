Orange-Ginger Tea

Description

This is a simple recipe I picked up in a restaurant I worked for. This tea requires fresh ingredients!

Ingredients

  • (bio) oranges
  • ginger
  • mint
  1. Cut the orange and mint in small piece and the ginger in stripes
  2. Bring water to a boil
  3. Put the ingredients into a glass and pour boiling water into it
  4. Voila! Serve with honey if you like!
